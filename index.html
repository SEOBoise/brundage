Explore the Best of Idaho Skiing at Brundage

Welcome to Brundage, your premier destination for unforgettable Idaho skiing adventures. Nestled in the heart of the mountains, Brundage offers pristine snow, epic slopes, and breathtaking alpine scenery that make every winter escape extraordinary.

From powder-packed trails to well-groomed downhill terrain, Brundage is a skier and snowboarder’s paradise. Our world-class resort features modern chairlifts, a cozy chalet-style lodge, and gear rental options that include top-quality boots, poles, jackets, and goggles — everything you need for a seamless day on the slopes.

Experience thrilling runs across diverse terrain, whether you’re carving alpine trails, exploring backcountry powder, or enjoying a family-friendly vacation. Our scenic lifts deliver you straight to adventure, while our après-ski amenities and welcoming mountain lodge provide the perfect place to relax after a cold, exhilarating day.

With easy access to nearby towns like McCall and just a short trip from Boise, Brundage is perfectly located for your next winter getaway. Whether you're planning a ski vacation, looking to rent gear, or seeking the best snow conditions Idaho has to offer, Brundage delivers the perfect blend of fun, excitement, and mountain charm.
